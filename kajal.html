How to Remove Kajal Correctly and Without Ruining It

Wearing kajal is one of the most distinctive and lovely aspects of Indian makeup. And, while we are constantly on the lookout for smudge-proof, waterproof, and long-wearing kajal or kohl pencils, we are all too aware of how difficult it can be to remove these products from the eyes. Because kajal or kohl is worn on the waterline of the eye, it is critical not to remove the product with harsh soaps or regular face cleansers. In this post, I'll discuss some simple, gentle, and effective methods for completely removing kajal from your eyes. The following are some gentle and effective methods for removing kajal from your eyes:

1.Coconut oil:

Coconut oil is one of the safest and most effective ingredients for removing all types of makeup, particularly eye and lip makeup. Ensure that you use virgin, cold-pressed olive oil. You can use a Q-tip or a cotton pad to gently press the coconut against the waterline. Allow a few seconds and then gently wipe away the product.

2.Olive oil:

Another nutrient-dense oil that can be used to remove makeup and moisturise the delicate eye area is olive oil. It is also recommended to use organic and extra virgin olive oil in this case, and to follow the same procedure as with coconut oil.

3.Cleansing milk:

If using an oil is not for you, regular cleansing milk will suffice. Using a cotton ball and a generous amount of cleansing milk, gently wipe away the kajal. Repeat this procedure 2-3 times more until the kajal is completely removed. Use an extremely soft cotton ball or pad.

4.Rose water:

Another extremely gentle and effective ingredient for removing eye makeup is rose water. It is completely painless and effectively removes all traces of kajal.

5.Petroleum jelly:

Similar to the good old Vaseline, petroleum jelly can be used to remove kajal residue from your eyes. Apply a small amount of Vaseline to your fingertip and massage along the waterline or wherever traces of kajal remain. Now, using a cotton pad, gently wipe away the product.

6.Raw Milk:

Yes, raw milk can be used to remove eye makeup. Indeed, you can apply raw milk to the entire face to brighten the skin tone. 10.Baby Oil: This is the most gentle eye makeup remover available. Apply a few drops of baby oil to a cotton ball and gently swipe away. Eyes should be rinsed with lukewarm water.

7.Almond Oil and Aloe Vera Gel Pack:

Utilize BB creams, kajals, and lip glosses that are lightweight. Utilize products that are gentle on the skin and will not cause breakouts when used regularly. Substitute rose water or cucumber mist for your alcohol-based makeup fixing product. This will impart a youthful, soft glow to the skin, and daily use will have no adverse effect.

8. Jojoba Oil:

This natural oil is excellent for acne-prone skin due to its non-clogging properties. To make a natural eye makeup remover with jojoba oil, combine equal parts jojoba oil and rose water and dip a cotton ball in. Apply a dab over the kajal and gently wipe away any remaining makeup
