7 Effective Makeup Remover Methods | Makeup Remover Alternatives

Removing makeup can be challenging, even more so if you are a beginner. You may not possess the makeup remover shown in a YouTube tutorial. As a result, here are the best methods for removing makeup. These makeup remover alternatives are easy to find in your home and are also quite affordable.

Coconut Oil As A Makeup Remover

Coconut oil is a household staple. It's an excellent makeup remover. All you need is a few drops of coconut oil, which you can warm between your palms and apply to your face. It instantly dissolves all your makeup, including waterproof mascara. Then, using a mild face wash, wash your face. Lauric acid is found in coconut oil and has been shown to have antimicrobial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties. Utilize cold-pressed virgin coconut oil.

Coconut Oil Skin Benefits:

  1. Coconut Oil is a natural emollient.

  2. It aids in the improvement of skin elasticity and collagen production, resulting in a more youthful appearance.

  3. Coconut oil contains lauric acid, which has antimicrobial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties.
  4. It is anti-aging in nature. However, I would not recommend coconut oil as a makeup remover for those with oily skin. If you have dry skin, combination skin, or normal skin, you should definitely try removing makeup with coconut oil. Additionally, it will nourish your skin.

Olive Oil Removes Makeup

For years, I've used olive oil as a body oil and makeup remover. Additionally, I've used it as a hair oil when my hair is particularly dry. They instantly remove waterproof mascara. All you need is a cotton pad and a mixture of olive oil and rose water (to dilute it), which you gently press onto your eyes. It instantly removes waterproof mascara in ten seconds. You can also apply a few drops to your hands and massage them into your skin to dissolve any makeup.

Olive Oil Skin Benefits:

  1. Olive Oil is a fantastic moisturiser.

  2. Maintains the health and radiance of your skin

  3. Relieves dry, patchy skin

Makeup Remover Lotion

Have you ever encountered a situation in which you were attempting to open a stubborn body lotion and it exploded in your face? That moment occurred for me, and as it turns out, it led me to discover a new makeup remover alternative. Thinner body lotions are ideal for removing makeup. My Ponds Triple Vitamin Moisturizer doubles as a makeup remover, effectively removing both foundation and lipstick.

Use Cold Cream As a Makeup Remover

Have you ever used Ponds Cold Cream to remove makeup? If not, then do so immediately. It is extremely hydrating while also removing makeup from the skin.

Vaseline As a Makeup Remover

I started wearing waterproof mascara and my Matte Me Liquid Lipsticks in high school, specifically 11-12 standard. At the time, I relied on Vaseline to remove waterproof makeup. All I had to do was scoop a small amount of Vaseline onto a cotton pad and swipe it across my lips and brows to remove the lipstick and mascara. It worked well, and I still use vaseline to remove my makeup when I forget to bring my trusty micellar water with me.

Vaseline has several applications:

  1. It can be used as a lip balm.

  2. It may be used as a natural luminizer.

Baby Wipes for Makeup Removal

I've removed makeup with baby wipes because they're relatively inexpensive. These are less expensive than makeup removing wipes. Baby wipes are typically very gentle on the skin and effectively remove makeup. Baby wipes are typically soap-free, alcohol-free, and gentle on the skin. They effortlessly remove foundation and lipstick, just like an actual makeup wipe. Baby wipes are significantly less expensive than makeup wipes.
